Sushi Recipes

Sushi is very delicious dish. But the fresh sushi are mostly have a high price. But, Don't worry!! With this recipe, you can make your own sushi to be your dishes. Let's try!

Ingredients

  • 11⁄3 cups of water

  • 2⁄3 cup uncooked short-grain with rice

  • 3 tablespoones rice vinegar

  • 3 tablespoones white sugar

  • 11⁄2 tablespoones salt

  • 4 sheets seaweed sheets

  • 1⁄2 pound imitation crabmeat, flaked

  • 2 tablespoones pickled ginger

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 300 oF (150 oC).
  2. Bring water to a boil in a medium pot; stir in rice. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and water has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.
  3. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl. Gently stir into cooked rice in the pot and set aside.
  4. Lay nori sheets on a baking sheet.
  5. Heat nori in the preheated oven until warm, 1 to 2 minutes.
  6. Center 1 nori sheet on a bamboo sushi mat. Use wet hands to spread a thin layer of rice on top. Arrange 1⁄3 of the crabmeat, avocado, cucumber, and pickled ginger over rice in a line down the center.
  7. Lift one end of the mat and roll it tightly over filling to make a complete roll. Repeat with remaining ingredients.
  8. Use a wet, sharp knife to cut each roll into 4 to 6 slices.

Recipe Tip

If you do not have a bamboo sushi mat, the easiest way to roll sushi is with a clean dish towel.
